Interests

My research interests are driven by a single fundamental question: Are we alone in the Universe? To help answer this question, my work focuses on Mars, which may have once hosted life (maybe it still does). Geologic and mineralogical evidence suggests that liquid water flowed across the Martian surface billions of years ago. Today, however, Mars is cold and dry and the atmosphere cannot support liquid water. So, the atmosphere must have been much different in the past. The climate may have been Earth-like for a long time. I'd like to help us better understand what that past climate was like, how long it was habitable, and what caused it to change.

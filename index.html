Marek Slipski

I am currently a NASA Postdoctoral Program Fellow at the Jet Propulsion Laboratory working with Dr. Armin Kleinboehl to create a comprehensive climatology of mesospheric clouds on Mars.

Previously as a graduate student and research scientist at the Laboratory for Atmospheric and Space Physics, I studied the evolution of Mars' atmosphere, the structure of its upper atmosphere, and processes that couple the lower, middle and upper atmosphere. I earned my Ph.D. in Geophysics from the University of Colorado Boulder, where I worked with Dr. Bruce Jakosky.
